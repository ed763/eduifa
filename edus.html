The Vision

Edu Ifa was initiated in 2023 by Eden Daba, a passionate high school innovator. The project began as a small offline quiz generator and evolved into the world's first Offline AI Learning Ecosystem.

Designed to run entirely without the cloud, Edu Ifa uses a lightweight on-device AI model built through intelligent JSON structures and algorithms that understand, explain, and evaluate students' work in real time—without using any online API.

Key Features

AI Tutor & Smart Quiz Master

Learners can input notes or upload lessons to instantly receive summaries, translations, and AI-generated quizzes.

Offline Class Meetings

Teachers and students connect via local Base6code or QR code—no internet required.

Voice-to-Text Inclusivity Engine

Converts speech into accurate notes, making learning accessible to all.

Gamified Learning

Students earn access to educational games only after achieving quiz goals, ensuring motivation and discipline.

Parental Control

Keeps families engaged by tracking progress and learning activities.

Offline Resource Library

Offers motivational materials that inspire consistency and ambition.

Innovative Technology

Edu Ifa's AI Tutor, Smart Quiz Master, Mind Map Generator, Trilingual Translator, and Teacher Connect tools all function through embedded local AI logic, proving that advanced technology can thrive without the internet.
